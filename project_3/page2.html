Music
Music Major Events
  • On January 9th, David Bowie performed a concert on his 50th birthday at Madison Square Garden, New York City with Frank Black, The Foo Fighters, Sonic Youth, Robert Smith (The Cure), Lou Reed, and Billy Corgan (The Smashing Pumpkins).

  • On March 10th, Marilyn Manson's concert in Columbia, South Carolina was canceled due to pressure from religious groups.

  • On April 14th, Depeche Mode came out with "Ultra," their 9th album.

  • On May 20th, The Foo Fighters releaseds their album "The Colour and the Shape.".

  • On October 15th, Michael Jackson ended the History World Tour, which had over 4,500,000 fans.

  • On November 19th, Michael Hutchence of INXS was found dead in a hotel room in Sydey, Australia.

  • On December 4th and 5th, Black Sabbath performed a pair of reunion shows in Birmingham, england. These concerts were the first full length concerts by the original lineup of the band since 1978.
