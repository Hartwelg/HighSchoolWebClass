 Notes for attendance:
If you know what your student will need to leave school early for an appointment, please send a note with them in the morning. your student will take that note to the attendance office in the morning and will be given a pass th leave class at the appropriate time. This helps assure your student will be let out on time an alleviate phone calls to the attendance office. If your student was out sick, please send a note with them when they return to school.

Dialer Phone Messages:
South High school has set up dialer systems to communicate important information to parents.The system will try to reach you three times. If you hang up immediately without listening to the message, the system recieves this as an incomplete connection and will try to dial your number again. Please stay on the line to recieve the full, important message.

No student check out after 2:00 pm without a note
Please remember that we do not check students out after 2:00 pm. unless a note has been sent with your student earlier in the day.

Notes are not delivered to classrooms
If your student has forgotten something or has asked you to drop off items for them in the office please remember that we do not deliver notes to the classroom. Have your student check in the Main Office if they have forgotten an item or expect to have something delivered. To help insure items are picked up we will announce the names of students with items dropped off at the end of class a few times a day.

InTouch


All Students are assigned a password for the Intouch program. Freshman will receive a new password within the first few weeks as passwords do not transfer from Middle School. This program allows you to stay informed of your student’s grades and attendance. Ask your student their user name and password or bring picture ID to the Main Office to receive this information.
Greetings Saxons
We welcome you to an excellent school filled with Staff who Care about our students and their education. We welcome you to a school that takes Pride in serving our students, families and community partners. We welcome you to a school of Action which defines our daily commitment to being a “School of Excellence.” This will be the second year South has been on a seven period day. Our office structure providing services to support families will remain the same except for our counseling service model. Counseling assignments will be made alphabetically allowing for students to stay with the same Counselor throughout their high school career. We believe this model provides a greater opportunity to build a relationship with your student and more effectively meet their counseling needs. Families who have more than one student will benefit by having the same Counselor. See page 3 for alpha counselor breakdown. As we prepare to welcome approximately 1,900 students and 138 staff members, I want to share with you that while we have had challenging times, I am excited and motivated regarding what we can accomplish in the upcoming school year. With that said, the South High staff is committed to assisting our students in raising required reading and writing scores as measured by state tests. It will take our entire community of students, parents and staff working together to deliver success for all Saxons. “Be true to your school” and we will achieve our goals for 2012-2013. Once a Saxon, always a Saxon! We look forward to another great school year. Respectfully, David Phelps, Principal

Senior Photo Deadline

    South High is offering several options for senior yearbook pictures. Digital format will be accepted this year. School ID pictures may also be used for the photo in the senior section unless you choose a photographer to provide us with a 2x3 photo by October 31, which meets the following guidelines:
  1. 2x3 glossy or semi matte (no texture) picture in color (name in pencil on the back)
  2. No borders
  3. Head size 1-¼ to 1-½ from top to bottom of chin
  4. Leave 1/8” between top of head and edge of picture
  5. Head and Shoulder format only
  6. No projected background, full length, hats, misty, plants, pets, double exposure or hands included.
  7. Proper color balance - Pictures need to be in color (no black and white pictures)
    8.


We will try to substitute a school picture for any studio photo that does not meet these guidelines.

Saxon Start Up Day
All currently enrolled students need to attend Saxon Start-Up Day to take care of business for the 2012-2013 school year. We follow an open format where students can come at anytime between the hours of 9:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. Students who cannot attend on this date will complete their business on their first day of school. Look for information packets mailed to homes in early August.

Remember to Bring:
all forms from the packet, including: • Completed Salem-Keizer Registration form (if your address has changed, please bring proof of new address such as a utility bill). • LIFeTOUCH School Picture form and a check if ordering photos • School Vehicle Registration Form (all licensed drivers must complete this form regardless of whether you park in the student parking lot or not).
